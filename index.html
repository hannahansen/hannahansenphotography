About Me

My name is Hanna Hansen! I have been a photographer on-and-off for 9 years. In March 2022 I officially started my little photography business and I haven't looked back! I love photoshoots & meeting new people! I love editing & I love being a part of special occasions!

I am a part-time photographer, full-time student studying software engineering (graduate December of 2024), and a data engineer at a local insurance company!

I love traveling, sports, good food, music, friends, family, and so much more!

If you are looking for someone who can work alongside you, your family, and/or your friends then you have come to the right place! I will always make sure that everyone feels comfortable around me while also making sure you get top notch photos!
